President - Michael D. Green

Michael D. Green is the Founder and President of Grenitaus Consulting LLC. Michael is a native Texan who received his B.S. in Management Information Systems from The University of Texas at Arlington in 2001.

Michael has over 16 years of experience in the IT industry as a Software Developer, Lead Developer, Architect, SCRUM Master, Consultant and Process Improvement Agent.

Primarily focused on Microsoft Technologies, Michael has experience in industries such as Marketing, Mortgage, City Government, Consulting, Healthcare and Retail.

Using technologies and methodologies such as Angular, ASP.Net MVC, ASP.Net Web API, SQL Server, TDD, Agile SCRUM and Azure, Michael has helped many companies implement solutions for the Front-End, Database, Process Improvement and the Back-End.

Michael is a Certified SCRUM Master, PMP holder and avid blogger who has written for several consulting companies, Medium.com and also maintains his own blog.
